Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Discord bots and applications operated by ukbigdave process information when used in Discord communities.

Last Updated: 27 June 2026

1. Services covered

This policy applies to Brock, DaveBot, Decko, DMforHelp, Nio, PoGo Raids, Pokémon GO Raids, Premier raids, PremierBot, PremierRaidsStaff, and The Raiders Club under ukbigdave.dev.

2. Information the bots may process

Depending on configuration and use, Brock and Nio may process Discord user IDs, server IDs, channel IDs, role IDs, message IDs, configuration data, moderation data, raid sign-up data, raid scheduling data, and limited message content where necessary for features.

Nio is a raid bot for organising Pokémon GO raids and stores raid information needed to organise and operate those raids. Brock provides Pokémon GO support, role assignment, moderation features, and raid logs, which are regularly purged.

The other bots covered by this policy do not store user data, but may transiently process Discord information when Discord sends an interaction, command, or event needed to respond.

3. How information is used

Information is used to provide bot features, organise Pokémon GO raids, store server configuration, support moderation workflows, assign roles, keep raid logs where applicable, respond to commands, maintain service integrity, troubleshoot issues, and protect against abuse.

4. Sharing and sale of data

Data is not sold. Data is not shared with third parties except where required by law or where necessary to comply with valid legal obligations.

5. Data retention

Data is retained only as long as required for operation, raid organisation, moderation, security, troubleshooting, or legal obligations. Retention periods may vary depending on the bot, feature, and type of information involved.

6. Data deletion requests

Users may request deletion of data where applicable by contacting privacy@ukbigdave.dev. Please include relevant details such as your Discord user ID, the server involved, and enough information to help locate the relevant data.

Some information may be controlled by server owners or administrators, may no longer be available, or may need to be retained for moderation, security, troubleshooting, or legal reasons.

7. Server owner and administrator control

Server owners and administrators control how the bots are configured within their communities. Their choices may determine what features are enabled, what data is logged, and how moderation or raid features operate.

8. Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy can be sent to privacy@ukbigdave.dev.
