Bot availability

Thanks for your interest

Discord bots operated through ukbigdave.dev are private community bots and are not currently available for installation on additional Discord servers.

Install requests are closed

Thank you for wanting to use one of these bots. At the moment, the bots are already operating at capacity supporting the servers they were built for, and ukbigdave does not have the time or support resources to expand them to additional communities.

For that reason, there are no public invite links, waitlists, paid exceptions, or manual approval options available. This is a firm availability limit rather than a negotiable application process.

Why this policy exists

The bots are maintained as a hobby and community project. Keeping them limited to their current servers helps protect reliability, moderation quality, privacy expectations, and the time needed to maintain the services responsibly.

Need help?

For privacy, support, or legal questions about the existing services, contact contact@ukbigdave.dev.
